About

_________________________________________________________

PetMatch focuses on helping individuals find their perfect pet companion so the owner can have a new best friend and so the pet can find their forever-home. We use special technology that filters out shelter databases by the preferences you enter so that an animal in need of a home that matches what you're looking for will instantly be recommended to you at your ease. Not only do we offer preference filters based on breed and species, but we also consider special needs, like those that may require service pets or animals with specific personalities. We also attach important information, like bite history and illnesses, on the pet so you will know what to expect when you meet your new friend for the first time, as well as how to approach your pet to form a lifelong bond. We use different technologies like Google Cloud API's, such as Maps JavaScript API and we're working to add features including AWS' machine learning software: Facial Rekognition.

The Team

Our team is made up of four individuals who have a passion for helping animals. At a TechTogether hackathon hosted in Boston, we met each other and decided that a great way to make an impact in a community that really needs it is by collaborating with each other and combining our coding knowledge with our desire to learn.
